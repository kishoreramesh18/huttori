About TAEKWONDO ASSOCIATION TAMILNADU

Taekwondo Association Tamilnadu is a state-level body dedicated to developing and promoting Taekwondo across Tamil Nadu. It functions under the framework of India Taekwondo and is affiliated with the Sports Development Authority of Tamil Nadu (SDAT). The association conducts state championships, training camps, and certification programs to support athletes, coaches, and referees. Through its structured development pathway, TATN enables athletes from Tamil Nadu to participate in nationally recognized competitions and advance toward higher levels of the sport.
