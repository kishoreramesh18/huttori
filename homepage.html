About TAEKWONDO ASSOCIATION TAMILNADU

The Taekwondo Association Tamil Nadu (TAT) is the officially recognized governing body for Taekwondo in the state. It is recognized by the Sports Development Authority of Tamil Nadu (SDAT) and affiliated with India Taekwondo. The association works to advance Taekwondo through state championships, athlete development initiatives, referee training, and official representation at national-level sporting platforms.

TAT operates from Room No. 78, Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium, Chennai – 600003, under the leadership of President P. Socratees, Working President S. K. Bharanitharan, Secretary Siddheswaran K.R., and Treasurer S. Kothandan. They oversee district Taekwondo units and prepare athletes for major competitions including Asian Games and the Olympics.

TAT maintains active communication through its website and social media platforms, sharing updates on tournaments, athlete achievements, and events such as the Khelo India National Championships.
