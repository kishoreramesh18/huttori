About TAEKWONDO ASSOCIATION TAMILNADU

Taekwondo Association Tamilnadu is a state-level body dedicated to developing and promoting Taekwondo across Tamil Nadu. It functions under the framework of India Taekwondo and is affiliated with the Sports Development Authority of Tamil Nadu (SDAT).
